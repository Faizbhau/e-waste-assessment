E-Waste Component Catalog

This catalog provides information about different types of e-waste components that can be assessed using our system.

Circuit Boards

Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are the foundation of electronic devices, connecting electronic components.

  • Common in: Computers, phones, TVs
  • Reusability factors: Condition, component density
  • Average quality score: 0.75
Processors/CPUs

Central Processing Units (CPUs) are the brain of computers, performing calculations and instructions.

  • Common in: Computers, servers
  • Reusability factors: Age, pins condition
  • Average quality score: 0.62
Memory Modules

RAM and other memory modules store data temporarily or permanently in electronic devices.

  • Common in: Computers, game consoles
  • Reusability factors: Corrosion, contact points
  • Average quality score: 0.81
Power Supply Units

Power Supply Units (PSUs) convert mains electricity to usable power for electronic components.

  • Common in: Desktop computers, servers
  • Reusability factors: Capacitor condition, output stability
  • Average quality score: 0.59
Storage Drives

Hard drives, SSDs and other storage media used to store data permanently in digital devices.

  • Common in: Computers, external storage
  • Reusability factors: Read/write cycles, physical damage
  • Average quality score: 0.72
Display Panels

LCD, LED, and other display technologies used in monitors, TVs, and mobile devices.

  • Common in: Monitors, TVs, phones
  • Reusability factors: Dead pixels, brightness uniformity
  • Average quality score: 0.48
